London

London is one of my favourite places to go in my holiday. Only 2 hours flight from Helsinki and you are in Heathrow. It is pretty easy to get anywhere from Heathrow by using the underground or "tube" as the locals call it.

Few places where you definitely want to go in your trip to London:
  • London Eye
  • Big Ben
  • Buckingham Palace
  • Trafalgard Square
  • Picadilly Circus

Budabest

Budabest is a beautiful old city in Hungary. The Budabest Metropolitan Area is home to 3.3 million people.
Some facts about Budabest
Fact More details
The Budabest Zoo is one of the oldest zoos in the world. The Budabest Zoo opened its doors in 1865
Budabest is the biggest city in Hungary 20 % of Hungary's population lives in Budabest
Budabest has the oldest subway line in mainland Europe The line opened in 1896 in the year when Hungary celebrated its 1000th anniversary
